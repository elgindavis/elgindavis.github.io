Hey there! I'm Elgin and I'm a multidisciplinary problem-solver. My passion is creating experiences that complement the natural beauty and functionality of our world. I design, write code, and love engaging with fine arts!

I've spent the last few years working in software engineering and design internship roles with OpenClassrooms and Life.Church, and have experience in development (including for 3 of my own iOS apps), as well as conducting user research, wireframing ideas, and prototyping solutions. As a UX Designer, I use the knowledge gained from these experiences to better research, define, and understand problems using the design-thinking process, and to design experiences and solutions that meet the needs of the end user.
