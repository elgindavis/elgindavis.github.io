About

I'm Elgin Davis and I'm a Computer Science Major at Harvard University. My main career interests are in the areas of educational technology and humanitarian assistance. I love jazz and hip hop music, learning languages, and empowering people with the ability to create.

I want to improve the way we educate ourselves, starting with our primary and secondary education systems. The power to create and express should not be reserved for those with exclusive access to expensive equipment or a special education program. My dream is to make knowledge more accessible.

SubSpace



SubSpace was created as a final project in CS50 by myself and Jack Kelley. The purpose of SubSpace is to allow students to access the college's official events calendar, while also having the ability to submit events to be added to an unofficial student calendar. It makes use of a Google-powered campus map to locate the events. Now you can stay on top of all of the Harvard-sponsored events, while also creating and sharing "low-key" events within the student body.
