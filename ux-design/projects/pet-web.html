TeleVet B2C Web App

Software Engineer, Designer, Accessibility and Usability Leader

A quick-to-market, React-based MVP web app

MVP web app for pet parents

Role/Context

In this project, I worked alongside a team of engineers, a contracted designer, and a contracted project manager. Over the course of 3 months, we worked to build a quick-to-market B2C minimum viable product web application to serve pet parents whose veterinarians are clients with TeleVet. My role was mostly software engineering, but I helped out significantly with design and usability.
*Visuals are not able to be displayed

Process

  • After the initial designs were ready from the designer, I analyzed the designs and drafted a detailed, actionable document noting some key accessibility, usability, and user experience concerns with the designs, and suggested improvements that were able to steer the design process in a more solid direction.
  • I also led the UI/UX implementation in the engineering team to help the project reach MVP stage and get the project out to users.

Challenges

There were some challenges working with the contracted designer, and ultimately our team decided to end the contract before the project was completed. After this event, I led most of the user experience decisions and the UX/UI implementation for the launch of the MVP web application.

Outcome

At the time of writing (2 months post-launch), the app has been released to all users and we’ve had 0 reported issues with accessibility or usability. According to our customer success team and analytics tools, the users have been able to easily navigate the application and get care for their pets efficiently. The team overall did a really great job getting everything done and launched, and I’m really grateful that I had the opportunity to lead the design efforts for such a big project so early in my career.
